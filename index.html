Who We Are

At Ciccone, Coughlin & Waldman, we use our knowledge of the law and top-notch litigation skills in order to give our clients the most comprehensive legal services. We have achieved success in and out of the courtroom by providing our clients with unparalleled legal representation, personal attention, and dedicated advocacy. We have a proven track record of exceptional settlements and verdicts.

Richard A. Ciccone, Esq.

Richard A. Ciccone received his undergraduate degree from the University of Rhode Island in 1963 and his law degree from St. John's University School of Law in 1967. He has been a member of the Bars of the State of New York since 1967 and the State of Rhode Island since 1967.

Attorney Ciccone has extensive trial experience in the Rhode Island District Courts and the Rhode Island Superior Court as well as appellate experience before the Rhode Island Supreme Court.

Attorney Ciccone has prepared and tried cases in the areas of criminal defense, as well as civil litigation related to automobile accidents, workers' compensation, medical malpractice, products liability, defective products, wrongful death, and general negligence.

John S. Coughlin, Esq.

John S. Coughlin received his undergraduate degree from Providence College in 1978 and his law degree from Brooklyn Law School in 1981. He has been a member of the Rhode Island and Massachusetts Bars since 1981.

Attorney Coughlin has extensive trial experience in the Rhode Island District Courts and the Rhode Island Superior Court.

Attorney Coughlin has prepared and tried cases in the areas of criminal defense, including felonies, misdemeanors, DUI, sexual assault and illegal drug cases, as well as civil litigation related to automobile accidents, workers' compensation, medical malpractice, products liability, defective products, wrongful death, and general negligence.

As a Superior Court Arbitrator and Mediator, Attorney Coughlin has mediated and arbitrated over 10,000 cases.

Paul F. Waldman, Esq.

Paul F. Waldman, after receiving his law degree from the University of Toledo, worked as a staff attorney for the Interstate Commerce Commission in Washington, D.C., from 1967 to 1969. Attorney Waldman has been associated with the Law Firm of Ciccone, Coughlin and Waldman for the last twenty-three years, primarily representing clients in the areas of personal injury, bankruptcy and probate matters, in both State and Federal Courts.

Andrew J. Coughlin, Esq.

Andrew J. Coughlin, a member of both the Rhode Island and Massachusetts bars, received his undergraduate degree from the University of Connecticut (Storrs) in 2010 and his law degree, Cum Laude, from Roger Williams School of Law in 2013.

While a law student, Attorney Coughlin was selected to participate in a Judicial Externship for the Honorable Daniel A. Procaccini of the Rhode Island Superior Court where he was able to gain invaluable experience in both Civil and Criminal trials.

Attorney Coughlin represents clients in various fields ranging from criminal defense to personal injury, workers compensation, and family law.
